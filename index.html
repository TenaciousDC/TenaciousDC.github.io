Adventures and Attractions!

When visiting Spain, you can enjoy a wide array of experiences, from historical sites and vibrant cities to delicious food and stunning natural landscapes. The top 5 are:

  1. Sagrada Familia (Barcelona): Antoni Gaudí's unfinished masterpiece, a breathtaking basilica.
  2. Alhambra (Granada): A stunning palace and fortress complex showcasing Moorish architecture.
  3. Prado Museum (Madrid): Home to a vast collection of Spanish art, including works by Goya and Velázquez.
  4. Retiro Park (Madrid): A large and beautiful park in the heart of Madrid, perfect for a relaxing stroll.
  5. Park Güell (Barcelona): Another Gaudí creation, a whimsical park with unique architectural elements.
